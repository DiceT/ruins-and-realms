# Sword & Shield — Manoeuvre Table

*The defensive specialist. Balance of offense and defense.*

---

## Style Philosophy

```
PRIMARY DIE   = Sword arm (attack, thrust, slash)
SECONDARY DIE = Shield arm (block, bash, position)

HIGH PRIMARY, LOW SECONDARY → Sword leads, aggressive
LOW PRIMARY, HIGH SECONDARY → Shield leads, defensive
BALANCED (4-5 range)        → Measured stance, versatile
```

### Special Mechanic: Shield Defense

Your **Secondary die value** from your attack sets your shield position for the round. If an enemy attacks with that **Primary value**, you block/reduce incoming damage.

*Example: You roll [5,7]. Your shield is "set" at 7. Enemy attacks with [7,3] — blocked!*
