# The 8 Ruin Domains (Locked Set)

These Domains represent the primary *Ruin-scale* locations in **Ruins & Realms**.  
Each Domain absorbs many dungeon “types” via synonyms and defines a distinct mode of exploration, risk, and Realm impact.

---

## 1. Castle
**Core fantasy:** Authority, defense, command  

**Synonyms:**  
Castle, Keep, Fortress, Stronghold, Bastion, Bulwark, Citadel, Redoubt, Palace

**Realm impact:**  
- Military capacity  
- Command structure  
- Legitimacy and authority  
- Organized defense

---

## 2. Temple
**Core fantasy:** Faith, doctrine, taboo  

**Synonyms:**  
Abbey, Cathedral, Chapel, Shrine, Sanctuary, Sanctum, Monastery, Pagoda

**Realm impact:**  
- Cohesion and belief  
- Ritual and religious infrastructure  
- Ideological influence  
- Magical or doctrinal leverage

---

## 3. Ruin
**Core fantasy:** Age, collapse, forgotten purpose  

**Synonyms:**  
Ruin, Edifice, Cenotaph, Mausoleum, Obelisk, Sepulchre, Ossuary, Grave

**Realm impact:**  
- Knowledge recovery  
- Relics and lost techniques  
- Historical leverage  
- Long-term consequences

---

## 4. Mine
**Core fantasy:** Extraction, labor, depth  

**Synonyms:**  
Mine, Quarry, Delve, Pit, Depths, Chasm, Fissure

**Realm impact:**  
- Material resources  
- Industry and production chains  
- Crafting capability  
- Economic expansion

---

## 5. Dungeon
**Core fantasy:** Confinement, coercion, suffering  

**Synonyms:**  
Prison, Dungeon, Hold, Pen, Panopticon, Warren (coercive)

**Realm impact:**  
- Population origin and growth  
- Loyalty narratives  
- Manpower and labor  
- Justice and order structures  

*Ideal starting Ruin Domain.*

---

## 6. Nest
**Core fantasy:** Infestation, occupation, reproduction  

**Synonyms:**  
Nest, Lair, Den, Burrow, Warrens, Web, Hive

**Realm impact:**  
- Threat reduction  
- Regional safety  
- Pressure relief over time  
- Ecological stability

---

## 7. Cavern
**Core fantasy:** The wild underworld, untamed depth  

**Synonyms:**  
Cavern, Caves, Grotto, Chasm, Depths, Fissure

**Realm impact:**  
- Territorial expansion  
- Access routes  
- Environmental mastery  
- Unknown ecologies

---

## 8. Catacomb
**Core fantasy:** Death, legacy, the layered dead  

**Synonyms:**  
Catacomb, Crypt, Tomb, Vault, Treasury Vault, Necropolis, Ossuary, Sepulchre

**Realm impact:**  
- Ancestry and inheritance  
- Curses and funerary rites  
- Legacy claims  
- Death-bound economies

---

**Notes:**
- All Domains are hostile, explorable Ruins.
- Layouts (Maze, Labyrinth, etc.) are features, not Domains.
- Each Domain provides distinct, significant Realm rewards.
- Aspect × Domain combinations drive variety and replayability.
